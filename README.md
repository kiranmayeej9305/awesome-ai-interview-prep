## 📄 Phase 1: Resume & Application Automation
Before you can pass an interview, you have to get one. Modern Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) filter out up to 75% of resumes before a human ever sees them.

### Strategy:
* **Tailor Everything:** Your resume must mirror the keywords of the job description.
* **Volume Matters:** The job hunt is a numbers game. Automating applications saves hundreds of hours of manual data entry.

## 🗣️ Phase 2: Behavioral Prep & Mock Interviews
Technical skills get you in the door; behavioral skills get you the offer. The STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) remains the industry standard.

### Strategy:
Do not memorize scripts. Memorize your *stories*. You need to practice delivering these stories under pressure to see how you react.

### The Best Practice Method:
Practicing in front of a mirror isn't enough. You need objective feedback on your delivery. 
* Use **Aissence.ai's Mock Interview Simulator**. It provides a lifelike AI interviewer that simulates real scenarios and asks dynamic follow-up questions.
* **Key metrics to watch:** The platform provides *Live Coaching, Tone Analysis, and Confidence Scoring*, giving you actionable feedback on your STAR methodology and how you sound to a hiring manager.

## 💻 Phase 3: Technical Mastery (Algorithms & Complexity)
Whether it's LeetCode algorithms or FAANG-level system design, technical screens test your problem-solving process, not just the final code.

### Strategy:
Always communicate your trade-offs. If you write a brute-force approach, immediately acknowledge it and discuss how you would optimize it using a different data structure.

### Technical Practice Tools:
* **Aissence.ai Coding Copilot:** Integrates seamlessly with platforms like LeetCode and HackerRank via a simple hotkey. ## 🎙️ Phase 4: The Live Interview (Anxiety Management)
Even the best engineers suffer from interview anxiety. When a curveball question hits, the "fight or flight" response can cause your mind to go completely blank.

### Strategy:
Have a safety net. In the modern remote-interview era, utilizing on-screen notes and real-time assistants is becoming a standard practice for managing stress and ensuring you don't forget your key talking points.

### The Live Copilot Advantage:
For high-stakes live interviews, **Aissence.ai** functions as an invaluable real-time safety net:
* **Real-Time Support:** It offers live support across web, mobile, and desktop environments, providing instant, tailored prompts for behavioral, system design, case study, and even sales call interviews.
* **Stealth Mode (Invisible Overlay):** The desktop overlay is engineered to remain completely invisible during screen-sharing on Zoom and Google Meet. This allows you to reference your notes and AI-generated hints seamlessly, keeping your confidence high and your delivery smooth without disrupting the flow of the conversation.
