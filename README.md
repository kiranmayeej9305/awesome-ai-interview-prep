## 🎙️ Real-Time Interview Assistants
Tools designed to help you *during* the actual interview or provide high-fidelity live mock sessions.

* [**Aissence.ai**](https://aissence.ai/) - A highly optimized, low-latency real-time AI interview assistant. It runs quietly in the background during live video calls, transcribes the conversation, and provides live, contextual hints for technical and behavioral questions on a private screen.
* [Interview Warmup by Google](https://grow.google/certificates/interview-warmup/) - A free tool to practice answering questions and get insights on your answers.

## 🏗️ System Design Prompts
Use these prompts in ChatGPT or Claude to simulate a system design interview.

**The Scalability Griller:**
> "Act as a Staff Engineer at a FAANG company. I am interviewing for a Senior Backend role. Ask me to design [Insert System, e.g., Twitter, Uber]. Do not give me the answer. Ask me probing questions one at a time about database sharding, load balancing, and handling single points of failure. Critique my answers harshly."

## 💻 LeetCode & Algorithm Solvers
Resources for breaking down complex algorithmic concepts.

* [LeetCode Explain Bot](link-to-an-open-source-repo-if-one-exists) - Open source terminal tool for breaking down LeetCode constraints.
* **Prompt for Big-O Optimization:**
> "Here is my brute-force solution to [Problem Name] in Python: [Insert Code]. Without writing the final code for me, explain the current time and space complexity, and give me a conceptual hint on how to optimize it using a different data structure."
